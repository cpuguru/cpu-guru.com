About

A small firm with a long record.

CPU Guru LLC was founded by Douglas A. White, a U.S. Navy Veteran (Nuclear Electronics Technician) who spent more than 27 years leading secure research computing and grant-funded IT at the University of Delaware before retiring in 2024. The firm pairs that depth with current Space Operations coursework from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University — serving research communities, federal programs, and operators of mission-critical systems.
